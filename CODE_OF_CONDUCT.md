# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and implementation tradeoffs.
- Give and accept constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Prefer clear reproduction steps and concrete evidence when reporting problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or
ban participants for behavior that violates this code of conduct. Enforcement
decisions are based on the impact of the behavior and the health of the project
community.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers through GitHub. If the concern
involves sensitive information, use a private channel where available.

This code is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
